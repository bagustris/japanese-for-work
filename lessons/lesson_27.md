## Cultural Notes

### Asking for Overtime in Japan

When asking someone to work overtime, especially part-time workers:

1. **Acknowledge the imposition** - Use cushion words likeã€Œã”ç„¡ç†ã‚’è¨€ã£ã¦ã€
2. **Apologize first** - Show you understand it's inconvenient
3. **Explain the situation** - Give clear reasons why it's necessary
4. **Be specific** - State exactly how long it will take
5. **Show appreciation** - Thank them sincerely

### Part-time Workers' Rights

- Part-time workers (ãƒ‘ãƒ¼ãƒˆ) have set working hours
- Overtime should be requested, not demanded
- Many part-timers have family responsibilities
- Respecting their personal time builds better relationships
