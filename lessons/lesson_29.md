## Cultural Notes

### Relaying Information About Illness

When reporting someone's illness to a superior:

1. **Be factual** - State what you observed and what they told you
2. **Use reporting forms** - ã€Œã€œã¨è¨€ã£ã¦ã„ã¾ã—ãŸã€ã€Œã€œãã†ã§ã™ã€

4. **Show concern** - But remain professional and objective

### Japanese Workplace and Health

In Japanese work culture:
- Employees often try to work through minor illnesses
- There's social pressure not to miss work
- However, infectious diseases require staying home
- Reporting health issues accurately helps supervisors make informed decisions
